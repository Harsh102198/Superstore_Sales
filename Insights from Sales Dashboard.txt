Insights from Sales Dashboard

Technology category leads in overall sales, contributing nearly 38% of total revenue, followed by Office Supplies (33%) and Furniture (29%) — showing strong demand for tech-related products.

Central and South regions are the top-performing regions, with combined sales crossing 4M+, while Canada and Caribbean show minimal activity — highlighting potential markets for growth or marketing focus.

Sales show a gradual downward trend over time (as seen in the Month-Year line chart), indicating either seasonal slowdown or reduced customer demand in later months.

The count of regions by sales chart reveals that most regions have lower sales counts, with only a few dominating, suggesting uneven distribution of performance across regions.
